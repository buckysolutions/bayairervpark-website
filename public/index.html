Welcome to Your Getaway Nestled on the Florida Gulf Coast

WHY CHOOSE US

  • Bay Aire is a 55 plus, gated, RV park community with 170 total RV sites accommodating RVs up to 42 ft.
  • Close to top-rated beaches, great restaurants, shopping, golf, and cultural activities.
  • Steps from the Pinellas Trail and a Jolley Trolly stop.
  • Only a short drive to Tarpon Springs and the sponge docks with a marina and boat launch across the street.
  • Enjoy our seasonal social activities or just relax in the heated pool!
