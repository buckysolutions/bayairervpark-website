About Us

Get to know our Park!

All sites are full hookup with 30 and 50 amp service available and free access to WiFi internet. Each site has a gravel base with a concrete pad to accommodate your RV stairs. We have a propane fill station on site for your convenience. A marina and boat launch is nearby, and we have limited storage sites available for your trailer and small watercraft!

Welcome to our Park - across the street and steps away from the beautiful gulf coast of Florida.

Relax on the patio of newly remodeled, heated, handicap-accessible pool.

Contact Us

Relax in our air conditioned clubhouse which includes a kitchen and library as well as full bathroom facilities and newly updated laundry facility.

Enjoy BBQs, theme parties and dances. Join a cornhole tournament, play shuffleboard or BINGO, or take an organized bike excursion along the Pinellas Trail.
