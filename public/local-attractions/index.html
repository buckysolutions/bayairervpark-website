Local Attractions

With nearly 365 days of sunshine, the Gulf Coast of Florida offers it all! Great beaches, access to both fresh and saltwater for boating and fishing, and lots of local attractions and restaurants.

Beautiful white sand beaches including Clearwater, Honeymoon Island and Dunedin.

Don’t feel like driving – enjoy the Clearwater “Jolley Trolly” which stops right at our Park entrance and can take you up and down the coast from Clearwater to Tarpon Springs!

Enjoy a stroll or bike ride on the lovely Pinellas Trail with access right in front of our Park and leading all the way to Clearwater.

Spend a day in historic Tarpon Springs and enjoy the sponge docks, restaurants and shopping.

Enjoy great local seafood at many area restaurants, launch your boat into the Bay, enjoy a show at the Ruth Eckerd Hall Performance Center or stroll area shops. This area has it all!
