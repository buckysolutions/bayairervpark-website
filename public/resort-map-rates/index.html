Resort map & Rates

MAY 2025 ‑ MAY 2026 RATES

All rates include water, electric, sewer, taxes and Wi-Fi. Sites are designed for 2 adults, 1 RV and 1 motor vehicle. Any additional vehicles must be parked in the designated overflow lot and may be subject to a fee. All rates are based on two-person occupancy. There is an additional fee of $5.00 per person per day. Other fees may apply - please refer to the Park office. No refunds for early departures. Rates are subject to change without notice.

A security deposit is required at check in for all stays of 6 months or longer.
